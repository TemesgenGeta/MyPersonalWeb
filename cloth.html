Best Ethiopian Traditional Wedding Cloths
for Men Women
CONNECT WITH ME

In Ethiopia, most of the weddings take place after the fasting season
known as Abey Tsome, which is the long lent offering that usually ends
around the first of May.

Those Ethiopian or non-Ethiopian brides to be usually begin the long
process of creating their perfect Ethiopian traditional wedding dress or
clothes about a month or two before the actual wedding date.

Although long, the process is actually exciting and fun for those partaking
in the activity. The beautiful and vivid colors to choose from, the material,
the actual design, etc. are the process for creating the best traditional
Ethiopian wedding clothes for herself and most often for her husband to be.

If one lives in Ethiopia, then the go to stop would be a place called Shiro Meda,
where most traditional Shemane or weavers have their shops.

One would then pick the perfect material/textile, then do the obvious window
shopping, be creative and create a design that suits you then pick and choose a
shemane that can make it happen. This is the basis which makes up the exciting
process of creating one of the best Ethiopian traditional wedding clothes.
